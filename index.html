Neural Computers

Zhuge et al. (Meta AI, KAUST) · April 2026 · arXiv:2604.06425

Can a single neural network replace a physical computer's runtime? This paper introduces the Neural Computer (NC) abstraction and two prototypes: NC_CLIGen for terminal simulation and NC_GUIWorld for desktop GUI. We found a verified novel domain contribution in CLI simulation and a decisive training insight (SVG cursor supervision: 8.7% → 98.7%), alongside significant gaps in closed-loop evaluation and citation coverage.
